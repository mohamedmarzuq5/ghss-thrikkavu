Government Higher Secondary School Thrikkav is a government school located in Thikakkav area of Ponnani sub-district in Tirur education district of Malappuram district. Commonly known as Trikkav School, established in 1893, this school is one of the oldest schools in Malappuram district.

It is an educational institution that is more than a century old. It is the only government higher secondary school in Ponnani Municipality. Formerly the school has functioned under various names as Trikkav Girls School, District Board Higher Elementary School, and Govt. U.P. School. It was upgraded as high school on 18th September 1980 and Higher Secondary School in 2004. Today the school has classes from standard one to plus two.
